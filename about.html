About TrackAssam

Hello and Welcome!

We are absolutely thrilled to have you here. TrackAssam isn't just another news website; it’s a modern media project started by a passionate team right here in Assam. We know how frustrating it is to scroll through cluttered websites filled with fake news, clickbaits, and annoying pop-up ads just to read a simple update.

We wanted to change that. That’s exactly why we created TrackAssam—to build a clean, lightning-fast, and premium platform that delivers top-tier journalism and viral updates to the people of our state and beyond.

Our Mission

Our core mission is simple: To bridge the gap between local updates and global standard journalism. Whether it's a major infrastructure development in Guwahati, a viral trend on social media, or the latest AI revolution in the tech world, we want TrackAssam to be the first tab you open on your browser.

What We Offer

  • Trending News: Fast and accurate coverage of what's going viral across the internet.
  • Exclusive Assam Updates: Ground-level reporting and verified news that matters to the people of Northeast India.
  • Tech World: Deep dives into gadgets, software updates, and how technology is shaping our future.

Why Trust Us?

We believe in quality over quantity. Every story published on TrackAssam is cross-checked with official sources. Our website is specifically designed to be reader-friendly, ad-optimized (without ruining your experience), and perfectly readable on mobile phones. We respect your time.

Let's Grow Together!

This platform belongs to you as much as it belongs to us. If you ever have a news tip, a suggestion, or just want to say hi, please head over to our Contact Page. We read every single message!

Thank you for trusting TrackAssam. Keep reading, stay updated, and share the truth.
